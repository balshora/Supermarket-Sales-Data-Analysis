## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes historical sales data from a supermarket chain across different regions in Egypt. The main objective is to uncover actionable business insights regarding regional branch performance, product category profitability, and hidden market opportunities. 

By transitioning from raw data to a dynamic Power BI dashboard, this project empowers decision-makers to optimize cross-promotions, resolve underperforming categories, and ultimately maximize overall revenue.

## 💼 Business Problem
The supermarket chain is experiencing inconsistent revenue performance across its regional branches. 
Management requires a data-driven approach to identify top-performing regions, optimize product 
category strategy, and uncover hidden growth opportunities.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack Used
* **Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn):** Data cleaning, handling missing values, and initial Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
* **SQL (SQL Server / PostgreSQL):** Writing complex queries to aggregate sales, calculate revenue drivers, and rank regional performance.
* **Power BI & DAX:** Building an interactive dashboard to visualize trends, KPIs, and regional disparities for stakeholders.

## 🎯 Key Business Questions Answered
1. Which region generates the highest total revenue, and what drives its success?
2. What are the "Cash Cow" categories and brands within the supermarket?
3. Why is transaction volume high in certain regions while total revenue remains low?
4. How can we strategically leverage top-performing items to revive underperforming categories like Bakery & Produce?

## 🚀 Project Workflow
### 1. Data Cleaning & EDA (Python)
- Handled missing values and outliers using Pandas
- Performed statistical summaries and visual exploration with Matplotlib & Seaborn

### 2. SQL Business Analysis
- Aggregated regional sales and ranked branch performance
- Identified top revenue-driving categories and brands using CTEs and Window Functions

### 3. Power BI Dashboard
- Built interactive KPIs and regional comparison visuals
- Designed for non-technical stakeholders to explore insights dynamically

## 💡 Key Insights & Business Value (The "Aha!" Moments)
* **Regional Performance:** The **Cairo** branch is the most profitable, generating **59K EGP** due to high population density. Conversely, **Luxor** struggles with total revenue (**19K EGP**) despite a high transaction volume (**5K units sold**), indicating a need for up-selling strategies and low-cost essentials.
* **Top Revenue Drivers:** **Dairy Products** emerged as the primary category generating **66.18K EGP**. Additionally, the **Americana** brand proved to be a powerhouse ("Cash Cow") with **77K EGP** in brand equity.
* **Strategic Cross-Promotion:** Discovered that high-margin categories like **Bakery & Produce** are underperforming. **Recommendation:** Implement "Bundle Offers" pairing high-demand Dairy products with slower-moving Bakery items, and launch a "Freshness Guarantee" campaign to reclaim lost market share.
* **Market Expansion:** Partnering with top brands like Americana for exclusive seasonal promotions can draw more foot traffic and maximize ROI across all branches.
